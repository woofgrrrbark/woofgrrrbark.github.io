AC 26 PARKING LOTS ================== MY FAV: GRANT ST TRANSPORTATION CENTER ---------------------------------------- - can be quite crowded during the day, especially for the fursuit parade - owned by the city, 24h surveillance - best overall pricing - if you pull an all nighter at the waterfront, theres a chance the gates will just be open and you won't have to pay at all. not guaranteed though Day rates (5a-3:59p): < 2h .............. $6 < 4h .............. $8 < 6h .............. $10 6-24h ............. $13 Night/weekend rates (4p-4:59a): flat .............. $6 weekends .......... $6/24h RUNNER UP: ELEVENTH AND SMALLMAN + 1201 WATERFRONT PLACE ---------------------------------------- - both are even closer to DLCC than the grant st garage - overpriced if parking overnight Rates: < 6h .............. $8 < 12h ............. $12 < 24h ............. $24 BEST FOR AFTERNOONS: MELLON SQUARE + SMITHFIELD+LIBERTY ---------------------------------------- - both owned by the city, 24h surveillance - decent pricing during the day but not the cheapest ($20/24h) Rates: after 4pm-5:59am .. $8 all day Sat/Sun ... $8 BETTER BACKUP: THE PENNSYLVANIAN LOWER LOT ---------------------------------------- - no parking passes, plate based payment. no lost ticket fees - can be confusing to get out of for some - might be packed some days, shared with an office building Rates: < 2h .............. $6.49 < 3h .............. $9.49 < 12h ............. $15.49 weekends .......... $7.49 BACKUP: 1301 SMALLMAN ST ---------------------------------------- - private lot, restricted hours - decent pricing but the hours suck - cant stress enough how bad the hours are. use this for day trips only unless you want your car locked in there overnight Hours: M-F 6am-8pm Sat 8am-8pm Sun 8am-4pm Rates: flat rate ......... $10 ==================
